### Documentation Phases Explained

#### Phase 1: Foundations and Rationale
Establishes the conceptual basis for post-identity interaction, explaining why demographic-free cognitive modeling is necessary and how it differs from traditional approaches.

**Key Topics:**
- The problem with identity-based AI
- Cognitive-structural modeling principles
- Theoretical foundations
- Research motivation

#### Phase 2: Whitepaper Structure
Outlines the high-level architecture and planning structure for the MindFirst Engine.

**Key Topics:**
- System design philosophy
- Component relationships
- Implementation approach
- Research roadmap

#### Phase 3: Technical Specification
The complete technical architecture of the MindFirst Engine, including all subsystems, interfaces, and operational details.

**Core Components:**
- **OS Profiler**: Extracts cognitive-structural signals
- **OS Interpreter**: Selects behavioral strategies
- **OS Stabiliser**: Maintains session continuity
- **M1E API Layer**: Exposes cognitive maps to tools
- **Response Generator**: Produces adapted outputs
- **Safety Mechanisms**: Enforces post-identity constraints

#### Phase 4: Governance and Ethics
Defines how MindFirst must be used, maintained, and expanded, including ethical constraints and governance structures.

**Key Documents:**
- Governance framework and decision-making
- Ethical use policies and prohibitions
- Licensing and usage terms
- Project metadata and versioning
